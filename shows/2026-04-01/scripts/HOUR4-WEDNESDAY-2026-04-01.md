### SEGMENT: THE RANT

Can we talk about April Fools Day for a second. I used to love this day. When I was a kid you put a rubber spider in someone's cereal and everyone had a good laugh. Simple. Elegant. Terrifying for your little brother. Now every company on the planet thinks they need to do an April Fools prank. Oh look. This airline is offering flights to the moon. This fast food chain made a perfume that smells like french fries. This tech company announced a product that is somehow dumber than their actual products. And the worst part is you cannot tell what is real anymore. A headline will say something completely absurd and I have to sit here for ten minutes trying to figure out if it is a joke or just... Tuesday. We have broken reality so badly that April Fools Day is now just a regular news cycle with slightly more self-awareness. I saw a headline this morning and I genuinely could not tell you if it was a prank or not. And I have been reading news professionally for longer than some of you have been alive. So happy April Fools Day. Trust nothing. Question everything. Which honestly is just good life advice regardless of the date. Here is some music you can trust.
