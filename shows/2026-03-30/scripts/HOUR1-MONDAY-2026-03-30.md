**[WKRP TRANSITION: 3 sec]**

> Okay. The rant. And I have been saving this one all weekend.
>
> Can we talk about the self checkout? I understand the concept. I do. You scan your items, you bag your items, you pay, you leave. Simple. Except it is never simple. Because there is always someone in front of you... trying to buy produce. And now they are scrolling through a touchscreen looking for the picture of a mango like it is some kind of fruit-based SAT question.
>
> And then the machine says unexpected item in the bagging area. And the light starts flashing. And now some eighteen year old kid has to come over with a key card to override the machine because your bananas offended it. And you are standing there, holding your one item, a bottle of water, wondering how we put a man on the moon but we cannot figure out how to sell a mango without human intervention.
>
> The future is here. And it is very, very broken.
