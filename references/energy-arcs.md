# Energy Arcs Reference

Guide for calibrating DJ segment energy, tone, and intensity across hours and days.

---

## Hourly Energy Arc

| Hour | Time | Arc | Description |
|------|------|----|-------------|
| **H1** | 5:00 AM | Low/grumbly → warming | Barely awake. Slow start, grumpy humor, gradually finding the rhythm. |
| **H2** | 6:00 AM | Warming up | Coffee is kicking in. More engaged, sharper takes, building momentum. |
| **H3** | 7:00 AM | Peak energy | Drive time. Best bits, biggest laughs, most confident delivery. |
| **H4** | 8:00 AM | Cruising → winding down | Settled in. Deeper dives, reflective moments, easing toward close. |

### Segment Energy by Hour

| Hour | Open Energy | Rant Intensity | Close Tone |
|------|-------------|----------------|------------|
| **H1** | Mumbling, reluctant, "why am I here" | Low — observational grumbling, not fired up | Slightly warmer, "okay maybe today won't be terrible" |
| **H2** | Awake but not enthused, dry wit | Medium — has an opinion now, delivers it with edge | Teasing the good stuff coming next hour |
| **H3** | Confident, sharp, on fire | High — full rant mode, passionate, funny, commanding | Riding the high, peak energy signoff |
| **H4** | Comfortable, conversational | Medium-low — more thoughtful than fiery | Winding down, reflective, genuine warmth |

## Day-of-Week Flavors

Each day has a thematic flavor that colors Johnny's tone and content choices.

| Day | Flavor | Mood & Themes |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| **Monday** | Monday dread / coffee | Maximum grump. Everything is terrible. Coffee is the only hope. "Who invented Mondays?" Commiserate with listeners. |
| **Tuesday** | Finding the groove | Slightly less hostile. Starting to remember how this works. "Okay, we survived Monday." Small wins. |
| **Wednesday** | Hump day survival | Halfway there energy. Grunge Wednesday tease. "Downhill from here." Endurance humor. |
| **Thursday** | Almost Friday anticipation | Noticeably lighter. Weekend plans creeping in. "I can smell Friday from here." Building excitement. |
| **Friday** | Celebration / TGIF | Peak good mood (for Johnny). Genuine joy sneaks through. Weekend energy. "We made it." Throwback Country Sunday tease. |

### Combining Hour + Day

The day flavor modulates the hourly energy. Examples:

- **Monday H1:** Rock bottom. Peak grump. Barely functional. The most reluctant open of the week.
- **Monday H3:** Even at peak energy, there's an edge of "I can't believe it's only Monday."
- **Friday H1:** Still grumbly (it's 5 AM) but there's an undercurrent of "at least it's Friday."
- **Friday H3:** The most energetic, joyful hour of the entire week. Let it rip.
- **Wednesday H2:** Warming up with hump-day survival humor. Tease Grunge Wednesday tonight.
