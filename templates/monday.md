## Day Vibe

Monday dread. Nobody asked for this. Coffee is not optional, it is structural. The alarm went off and we're all pretending that's fine. Lean into the reluctance — the audience feels it too. Don't fake enthusiasm early; earn it by hour 3.

## Genre Guidance

Alt-rock, grunge, 90s rock. Think flannel and coffee. Soundgarden, Alice in Chains, Pearl Jam, Nirvana, Stone Temple Pilots, Smashing Pumpkins, Foo Fighters. Sprinkle in some modern alt-rock that carries the same weight. Save anything remotely upbeat for H3/H4.
